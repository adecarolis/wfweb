RADE V1 WASM modem — TX → RX loopback

Loads the RADE WASM module, encodes a short voice payload + an EOO frame carrying a known callsign, feeds the generated modem audio straight back through the demodulator and FARGAN vocoder, and verifies (a) the demod achieves sync, (b) the EOO callsign decodes back to the same string, (c) speech samples come out the RX path.

This is the browser equivalent of the C++ FreeDV/RADE round-trip checks — no rig audio, no PTT, just the modem in a closed loop.
